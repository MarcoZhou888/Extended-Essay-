# Extended Essay: Example Commentary

**DP Subject:** Economics (subject-focused essay)  
**Interdisciplinary Framework:** N/A  
**Research Question:** To what extent did the UK government’s “Eat out to help out” scheme increase the consumption of restaurant food in Kent?  

---

## Assessment Details

### Criterion A: Framework for the essay
* **Mark awarded:** 6 / 6
* **Commentary:**
  * The research question is relevant to the topic of the investigation (government intervention), clearly stated and closely focused in relation to the scope of the essay. The geographic context of the essay is suitably focused on a particular area.
  * Research methods that are suitable for the research question are well explained and applied effectively. An effective blend of primary and secondary research is carried out, with a clear rationale provided for the choice of research methods. The approach of setting and testing a hypothesis works well here. While the scheme under investigation was a national one, the work makes a good effort to curate relevant local data.
  * Structural elements used in the essay effectively support the communication of the research:
    * Diagrams are well presented, with titles and figure numbers.
    * Footnotes are presented accurately, including *op cit* where appropriate.
    * Footnotes match the way that the sources are listed in the bibliography.
    * The bibliography is presented accurately making it easy to cross-check references.
    * Suitable data from the primary research is included in the essay, with supporting evidence in the appendix.
    * The table of contents demonstrates good organization of the essay, with appropriate sections and sub-sections, and these match the page numbers in the essay.

### Criterion B: Knowledge and understanding
* **Mark awarded:** 5 / 6
* **Commentary:**
  * Basic economics textbook theory combined with an appropriate range of primary and secondary research is used to establish and demonstrate knowledge of the subject matter.
  * Relevant terminology is used accurately and consistently to demonstrate understanding of the subject matter.
  * Relevant concepts are explained and used effectively to demonstrate understanding of the subject matter.
  * A notably good aspect of the use of economic theory is the effort to tailor the models to the case study, rather than simply provide the generic theory. This is observed where the work notes that the scheme “effectively acted as a subsidy” and moves to apply the subsidy theory appropriately.
  * Other concepts/theories that are explained and used are:
    * determinants of demand
    * supported and reasoned observations about price elasticity of demand
    * supported and reasoned observations about the role of income as a determinant of demand.

### Criterion C: Analysis and line of argument
* **Mark awarded:** 5 / 6
* **Commentary:**
  * Analysis in the essay is effective and consistently produces relevant findings. The work presents clear analysis of the research, directing the reader to the reasoned conclusions.
  * Although some points of the analysis are somewhat vague, there is a clear line of argument that largely links the research question, research findings and conclusion. Each section of the essay contains appropriate analysis and each section is linked to the next, indicating a logical and coherent argument.

### Criterion D: Discussion and evaluation
* **Mark awarded:** 7 / 8
* **Commentary:**
  * There is clear evidence of critical thinking throughout the essay, to provide a balanced discussion of the significance of the findings.
  * Evidence of this includes:
    * a clear rationale for the selection of sources
    * recognition of the need to corroborate sources
    * recognition and attempted measurement of other factors that could have had an impact on the market
    * an evaluation of the supply and demand model used in the analysis
    * an appreciation that “the data runs contrary to my hypothesis”
    * effective recognition and challenge of the *ceteris paribus* assumption
    * evaluation of the statistical significance of the data collected.
  * While it is notably good that there is a balanced and evaluative tone throughout the essay, the final brief conclusion marginally weakens the strength of the overall conclusions.

### Criterion E: Reflection
* **Mark awarded:** 4 / 4
* **Commentary:**
  * Reflection on the learning process is consistently evaluative and includes specific examples.
  * Reflection consistently shows evidence of growth.

---

## Final Assessment
* **Total marks awarded:** 27 / 30
* **Overall Examiner Comments:** This is a very strong essay. The work demonstrates effective research skills, resulting in a well-focused and appropriate research question that can be explored within the scope of the chosen topic. There is effective engagement with the research process. There is effective application of source materials and correct use of economics terminology.
