# Extended Essay: Example Commentary

**DP Subject:** Economics (subject-focused essay)      
**Research Question:** To what extent do the firms in the Singapore food delivery service engage in price and non-price competition?  

---

## Assessment Details

### Criterion A: Framework for the essay
* **Mark awarded:** 3 / 6
* **Commentary:**
  * The research question is relevant to the topic of the investigation but is only partially focused in relation to the scope of the essay. The research question is not suitable as a “to what extent” type of question. The firms in the industry practise price and non-price competition and it is not possible to measure the extent of either. (3–4 markband)
  * The research methods are suitable for some elements of the discussion, and are explained and applied with partial effectiveness. The bibliography suggests that a large range of secondary sources were used, but there is not a lot of evidence of these sources in the discussion. There is heavy reliance on some primary research based on consumer surveys, which do not generate significant results. There is virtually no explanation of the survey—for example, number of respondents or demographics of respondents. In the methodology, it is asserted that the secondary sources are credible, but this is not corroborated. (3–4 markband)
  * Structural conventions used in the essay support some aspects of the communication of the research. Most structural elements are correctly included—the table of contents, bibliography and appendices are all present and in the correct place in the essay. However, there are some aspects of the primary research that are in the appendix but would have been better in the main discussion (e.g. pricing data) and there are too many graphics included that do not add value to the discussion. There are some inconsistencies in the way in which references are cited in the text and in the bibliography. Together, these factors do not support the communication of the research. (3–4 markband)

### Criterion B: Knowledge and understanding
* **Mark awarded:** 3 / 6
* **Commentary:**
  * Some relevant research materials are used to establish some knowledge of the subject matter. The work uses basic textbook content to deliver some relevant theory. (3–4 markband)
  * Some relevant terminology is used accurately to demonstrate understanding of some of the relevant theory. (3–4 markband)
  * Some relevant concepts are explained and used with partial effectiveness. (3–4 markband)
  * Some of the relevant concepts include:
    * the basic characteristics of oligopoly
    * concentration ratio
    * price rigidity

### Criterion C: Analysis and line of argument
* **Mark awarded:** 3 / 6
* **Commentary:**
  * The essay includes some analysis that is partially effective and produces some relevant findings. However, it is based largely on primary data that is limited in its credibility. (3–4 markband)
  * The economic theory is used in a fairly descriptive manner. The diagrams and written theory are mostly hypothetical/theoretical rather than evidence based.
  * There are links between the research question, the research findings and conclusions. However, the limitation of the research question (as a “to what extent” question) makes it difficult to develop a consistent line of argument, and there is some confusion in the analysis of price and non-price competition, limiting the effectiveness of the conclusions. (3–4 markband)

### Criterion D: Discussion and evaluation
* **Mark awarded:** 3 / 8
* **Commentary:**
  * There is some discussion of the significance of the findings, sometimes supported by appropriate evidence. This would be better if the discussion occurred when the findings were presented. Some inconsistency occurs in the discussion concerning the sensitivity of consumers to price changes. (3–4 markband)
  * An evaluation of the effectiveness of the findings is present, with some strengths and limitations stated at the end, including some appropriate evidence. Nowhere is it recognized that the theory may not accurately reflect the actual behaviour in the market. (3–4 markband)

### Criterion E: Reflection
* **Mark awarded:** 2 / 4
* **Commentary:**
  * Reflection on the extended essay learning experience is descriptive and includes some examples. 
  * Reflection sometimes shows evidence of growth and transfer of learning.

---

## Final Assessment
* **Total marks awarded:** 14 / 30
* **Overall Examiner Comments:** This is a satisfactory extended essay. The chosen topic is relevant to the economic theory learned in class and the work attempts to fit it into a real-world example. The work demonstrates evidence of research undertaken, leading to a research question that does not really lend itself to a strong line of argument. There is partially effective engagement with mostly appropriate research areas, methods and sources. However, there are some discrepancies in those processes and some shortcomings in the research.
